Friends

Bushwalla

Bushwalla- A one man bohemia. A musician. A juggler. A fire-breathing
circus act. Bushwalla is an utilitarian humanitarian. His unique blend
of acoustic hip-hop sets a expressionistic backdrop that is as
enchanting as it is enlightened. Audiences won't soon forget this
performer extraordinaire.

Café Gratitude

Café Gratitude is an expression of a world of plenty. An organic, vegan, live food restaurant guised as a school of transformation, that supports local farmers, sustainable agriculture and environmentally friendly products??? We can get behind that. Practicing "Sacred Commerce: Business as a path to awakening" we've utilized many of this companies' philosophies to model our own. Be nourished and Be Love.

Daniel Kinno

A Russian born comedian, with a dissent born act, Daniel's clever and
self-aware political insight has landed him in front of troops in
Afghanistan as well as college students in Indianola, Iowa. He travels
the globe as a citizen of laughter, and is on a life long campaign for
sense in the world. Let him humor you.

Dave Matthews

Dave, at one point or another, has provided a signifigant portion the soundtrack to all of our lives. He wore the Love Fuel shirt on
stage at a show-which aside from the instant marketing of 30,000 fans at once, is just really really cool.

Ecorazzi

Ecorazzi catches celebrities green-handed. Turning the spotlight on celebrities who use their fame as a vehicle for activism and inspiring change. In the spirit of fair play, they're quick to point how who's slacking and who's deserving of recognition. They first caught up with us at the 2007 Chicago Green Festival. We wonder if they could use all those camera flashes for photosynthesis..?


Eric Hutchinson

A singer/soulwriter, Eric is one of the most dedicated musicians we
know. A one-man grassroots movement, Eric is up to big things. His
highly energetic live act, sarcastic banter, and heart felt optimism is
enrolling fans everywhere he and his music go. You've got to hear it to
believe it.

Justin Kredible

A Magician/Comedian/Most endearing guy you may ever meet. Like a magician, but so much cooler, and we'd say better. Blowing minds and erupting laughs, Justin is a regular correspondent on Rachel Ray, a featured magician on iD's "Turning Tricks," and has starred on MTV's Room 401. We'll let you in on one of his secrets: it's a Blend Apparel sleeve he's got a card up.

The Makepeace Brothers

This generation's Simon & Garfunkel. Timeless harmonies, you and your feet have sworn you've heard. The Makepeace Brothers are truly homegrown musicians. With lyrics channeled from a wiser time and place, they entrust their songs to grassroots wings. Listen along, and you'll be a peace making sibling in no time.

Jason Mraz

The biggest Blend Apparel supporter from the start,  so much so, Jason actually became a partner in the company. With music almost as heartfelt as his generosity, he strikes the most wonderful chords in anyone he or his music touches. Jason is touring the world as a prophet of love and inspiration. He's  methodically designed a free-styling lifestyle that enables him to share his lessons globally, and encourage kids of all ages to think forever young.

The Nova Project

A digital dynamoc duo. Video and documentarianism at its finest. These boys are birthing ideas into the world that have been gestating too long. Look novarther.
