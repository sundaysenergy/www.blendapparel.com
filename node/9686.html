Bun Apple Teet

Our first snack-themed tee -- Bun Apple Teet -- is glutten-free, organic, and practically naked. If the stomach is the way to one's heart, we have three options for you. Bun Appleteet! And check out your message on the backside of the hem tag -- you can show your friends a little sum'n sum'n . . .

Jason Mraz originally created this design as a painting that he gave to the person who inspired Song for a Friend, from the album, Mr. A - Z. And, like all of the shirts in this new line based on Jason's original designs, Bun Apple Teet is printed on what he calls a "hamper tee," his description for a tee he'll take out of the clothes hamper cause it's so comfee. Our "canvas" starts as an Alternative Apparel organic cotton, unisex tee. We then have our eco-friendly screen printin fools apply Jason's designs in H20 based inks (no plasitsol ink in the designs here at Blend, PVC or non-PVC).

Because each tee is as unisexee as it is comfee, they tend to run about a size smaller than a traditional shirt. Please make sure you click on the following link and check-out Alternative Apparel's sizing chart to ensure you're getting the unisexee size you wanna wear (while we have a generous exchange and return policy, we ask for -- and greatly appreciate -- your help in ensuring the least impact on our environment from shipping by confirming the sizing of your tee when ordering):
