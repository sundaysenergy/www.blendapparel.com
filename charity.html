Blend Apparel's Charitable Giving Philosophy

At Blend Apparel, we work every day following what we call the "good, better, best" approach to business. In doing so, we constantly evaluate our business practices in an effort to ensure as best possible that we have as little negative impact and as much positive impact on issues such as climate change, labor rights and the globalization of business. Whether that means exploring new and more environmentally friendly production or shipping methods, or simply turning-off our computers before finishing a day of work, it all plays a part. And, it all adds-up.

This philosophy carries over into our selection of recipients of our charitable giving. In selecting an organization to support, we try to find those that also follow a "good, better, best" philosophy. Our goal is to pick organziations that focus on the issues we hold dear, and to support them as best we can. Though Blend is young in age (born on Earth Day 2007), we felt good habits should start early. To that end, Blend Apparel has historically donated more than 1% (we call it 1%+) of its gross revenues to charity. This is our world -- it's Wear We Live. Thank you for helping us make it a better place!
