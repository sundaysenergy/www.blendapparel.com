Pause -- com ma (noun). 1. A punctuation mark; 2. A brief pause or interval; 3. A bold, new design for accelerated times reminding us to pause, take a breath, slow down, and reflect.

Like Jason's most cherished punctuation mark, our Pause tee is a reminder to remember the third definition. You'll get questions from many asking what this tee means ... when you do, pause for a moment and smile (and consider doing that more often, even if you're not wearing this tee). Then remind them that in these rapidly changing times, if we would all just pause for a moment, and think, we would likely find ourselves livin' in an even better world. Pause, and imagine that!
